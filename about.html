About us

Director

Paul has over 35 years of experience in the food and farming sector, with 14 years’ experience as an agri-food consultant establishing long term collaborative supply chains for both large and small food and farming businesses. He has a wide knowledge of the farming, processing and manufacturing sector and is skilled at delivering innovative solutions, managing change and making things happen. Paul is passionate about British Farming, the countryside and the wildlife supported by it.
